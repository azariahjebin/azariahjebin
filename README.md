## 🔬 &nbsp;Research & Publications

### 🧠 &nbsp;NeuroGraph — Predicting Pediatric Brain Tumor Biomarkers from H&E WSIs

> **Master's Thesis · University of Texas at Austin · December 2025**
> Supervised by Dr. Ying Ding & Dr. Leqi Liu · IRB Protocol STUDY00007710 · 182 cases · Dell Medical School

Pediatric brain tumors are a leading cause of cancer mortality in children. Molecular biomarker assessment via IHC, PCR, or NGS is expensive, time-consuming, and inaccessible in resource-limited settings. **NeuroGraph directly predicts these biomarkers from routinely available H&E whole slide images — no additional lab tests required.**

<div align="center">

| Biomarker | AUC | F1 Score | Accuracy | Signal |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---|
| 🟢 **GFAP** | **0.939** | **0.896** | 85.0% | Strong glial differentiation signal |
| 🟡 Synaptophysin | 0.777 | 0.648 | 68.2% | Moderate — balanced precision/recall |
| 🟡 ALK1 | 0.736 | 0.300 | 91.9% | Moderate — inflated by class imbalance |
| 🟠 H3K27M | 0.699 | 0.293 | 89.8% | Limited by sparse positives |
| 🟠 INI1 | 0.656 | 0.200 | 98.0% | High specificity on rare marker |
| ⭐ **Mean** | **0.761** | — | **86.6%** | Validated on real pediatric cohort |

</div>

**Architecture:**

```
Input WSI (1–5 gigapixels @ 40x)
  ↓  Tissue segmentation (HSV + Otsu thresholding)
  ↓  Patch extraction  →  256×256 tiles @ 20x  →  4,000–12,000 patches/slide
  ↓  UNI2-h foundation model  →  1024-dim embeddings per patch
  ↓  Delaunay Triangulation  →  Graph (400–6,000 nodes, pruned at 4,000μm)
  ↓  3× EdgeConv blocks (128→64→32 MLP, BatchNorm, GELU, residual connections)
  ↓  Global mean pooling  →  Slide-level embedding
  ↓  Classification head  →  Dropout (p=0.85) + Sigmoid
  ↓  Masked BCE Loss  →  Handles label sparsity across 5 biomarkers
  ↓  Predictions: [H3K27M, GFAP, Synaptophysin, INI1, ALK1]
```

**Key innovations:** Spatially-aware graph topology over standard MIL · Masked loss for sparse multi-label training · GNNExplainer for clinician-interpretable subgraph attribution · Custom clinical dashboard for pathologist-facing visualization · TACC HPC for feature extraction · NVIDIA A100 for GNN training

### 📊 &nbsp;Mitigating Dataset Artifacts in NLI: Sensitivity Analysis of Ensemble-Based Debiasing

> **Course Research · UT Austin · December 2025** · with Shobika A.

- Hypothesis-only ELECTRA-Small baseline exposed `60.12%` accuracy on SNLI — pure spurious artifact exploitation
- Product-of-Experts debiasing with tunable `α`: light (`0.2`) improves robustness · aggressive (`0.8`) degrades accuracy `89.6% → 87.9%`
- **Finding:** Debiasing is a double-edged sword — over-correction discards valid reasoning signals
